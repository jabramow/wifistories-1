About the Author

Jeff Abramowitz was an author of the original IEEE 802.11specification, a founder of Wi-Fi Alliance, and the founder/CEO of PowerCloud Systems (acquired by Comcast).  He has held leadership roles in the Wi-Fi industry for 25 years, and his oral history of Wi-Fi is chronicled in the Computer History Museum. In 2019, he was inducted into the Wi-Fi Now Hall of Fame. Jeff is a Principal at A2Z Partners, providing strategic counsel to tech executives.
